ABOUT ME

Here is a little background about me, and what brought me to this point.

Love in Creation

Storytelling has been my passion since childhood—an escape into the worlds of stories, plays, and poems. Creativity has been my guiding force, driving my love for creation and narrative artistry. Post-high school, struck with overwhelming uncertainty, one thing that remained clear was my desire to tell a story. I embraced my passion for dance and now am an aerial circus artist in my spare time. I infuse storytelling into my circus performances in every way I can. Each aerial act becomes a canvas where I blend character and narratives, inviting audiences to embark on a mesmerizing journey through storytelling in motion.

Passionate for Design

High school was a gateway to the world of design, where I plunged into inDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop. I used to be obsessed with recreating movie posters from online Photoshop tutorials. Stepping into the leadership role as president of the yearbook club, I clumsily orchestrated a team to craft our school's yearbook using inDesign. Now, in my design-focused classes at Camosun, I’m fully immersed—whether brainstorming solo or collaborating in teams, weaving together prototypes and wireframes. Design is where my creativity thrives, offering a canvas for exploration and innovative thinking.

Born Gamer

Video games have been my lifelong joy, starting from the days of Donkey Kong in the living room with my dad. Those early thrills led to diving headfirst into the fast-paced worlds of first-person shooters, where my Dad and I bonded over endless matches in Unreal Tournament 3. While competitive gaming isn't my cup of tea, I've found my groove in immersive RPGs and challenging encounters in MMOs. I tackle Heroic Raids and mythic plus dungeons in World of Warcraft, and continuously restart my Baldur's Gate 3 campaigns so I can try (and fail) at playing an evil character. I like to think I'm a pretty good Windwalker player, but I've been advised against linking my logs in my portfolio.

Eternal Explorer of Knowledge

After graduating, I initially pursued creative writing but shifted gears in search of a more dynamic field. I took a hiatus from formal education before the pandemic prompted my return, obtaining an office administration certificate. Hungry for ongoing challenges, I sought a career path that offered continual growth. Discovering the Interactive Media Developer program bridged my love for creativity and logic, prompting a bold leap to pursue this dynamic field - a decision fueled by my unwavering passion for constant learning.
