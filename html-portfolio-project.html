ABOUT ME

Here is a little background about me, and what brought me to this point.

2011-2015

Passion for Design Began

In highschool, I took a series of classes that taught me several Adobe programs, as well as GameMaker. In highschool, I was a part of the yearbook club, which required me to design yearbook pages using InDesign and Illustrator, and my passion for design was born.

2015-2020

A Little Lost

After graduating, I jumped straight into college and take a slew of creative writing courses. I enjoyed this, but my heart was not in it. I decided to take a break from school to figure out what I wanted to do with my life. .

2020-2022

Choosing a Course

After the first wave of the pandemic, I decided I needed a job that was more stable than what I was currently doing. In May of 2020, I decided to back to college to pursue a certificate in Office Administration.

2022-Present

Choosing the Right Course

In 2022, I finally discovered the course of my dreams, so I applied to Camosun to partake in the Interactive Media Developer Program, and am thouroughly enjoying it!
